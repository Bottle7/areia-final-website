About

Apartment living has its downsides. The noise caused by my partner's gaming chair was driving me crazy and the potential damage it might inflict on the floor was a concern.


Enter the chair mat.


I really liked the idea of having a chair mat to reduce the problems stated above; however, I've always thought that they looked very unappealing in pretty much every situation.


Areia chair mats are designed to be aesthetically pleasing and also provide the benefits of using other solutions.
